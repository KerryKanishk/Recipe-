DESCRIPTION

The dish typically features a simple sauce made from canned or fresh tomatoes, which are simmered with olive oil and garlic. The heat brings out the natural sweetness of the tomatoes and creates a rich, bright sauce. The sauce is then tossed with your favorite type of pasta, allowing it to cling to every noodle. The final result is a beautiful and tasty meal that is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner. Optional additions like fresh basil and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese can enhance the flavors and give it a more authentic, rustic feel.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 200g (about 7 oz) dry spaghetti or pasta of your choice
  • 1 can (400g or about 14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: Fresh basil, grated Parmesan cheese

RECIPE:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package directions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant (be careful not to burn it).
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes to let the flavors meld.
  4. the cooked pasta and add it directly to the skillet with the sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
